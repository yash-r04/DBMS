How it Works

Our system is designed for labs to streamline equipment management.
Admins can add equipment and suppliers. Staff can approve borrow requests. Viewers can request equipment and track borrowed items.

Role Overview

Admin: Manage all equipment, suppliers, and analytics.
Staff: Approve requests and track borrowed items.
Viewer: Request equipment and check availability.

Map of Events

Step 1: Register/Login

Sign up and log in to access the system according to your role.

Step 2: Request Equipment

Viewers submit a request for the equipment they need.

Step 3: Approval

Staff reviews requests and approves or rejects them based on availability.

Step 4: Borrow/Return

Once approved, the equipment can be borrowed and later returned, updating inventory.

Step 5: Track & Alerts

All roles can view usage, alerts, and equipment status in dashboards.
